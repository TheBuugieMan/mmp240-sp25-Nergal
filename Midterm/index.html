They say whatever doesn’t kill you makes you stronger, but that’s not the case for the world’s weakest hunter Sung Jinwoo. After being brutally slaughtered by monsters in a high-ranking dungeon, Jinwoo came back with the System, a program only he could see, that’s leveling him up in every way. Now, he’s inspired to discover the secrets behind his powers and the dungeon that spawned them.

S1: Solo Leveling

Episode 1 – I’m Used to It

Sung Jinwoo, known as the weakest hunter, joins a low-rank raid in a mysterious dungeon. Things go horribly wrong when a hidden double dungeon activates.

Episode 2 – If I Had One More Chance

Jinwoo and the surviving hunters face a deadly trial. In the face of death, he accepts a strange offer only he can see—a system that grants him quests and power.

Episode 3 – It’s Like a Game

Jinwoo awakens in the hospital and begins to notice changes in his body. The system pushes him into daily training quests that force him to grow stronger.

Episode 4 – I’ve Gotta Get Stronger

Driven by determination, Jinwoo enters a dangerous instant dungeon alone to level up. He begins to understand the mechanics of the mysterious system.

Episode 5 – A Pretty Good Deal

Jinwoo clears the dungeon and earns new weapons. As his stats rise, he becomes noticeably more powerful—and starts drawing attention from others.

Episode 6 – The Real Hunt Begins

Teaming up with a new raid party, Jinwoo heads into another dungeon. This time, the tables turn as he's no longer the weakest one in the group.

Episode 7 – Let Someone Else Do It

Jinwoo tests the limits of his new abilities, experimenting with stealth and summoning skills. He begins to realize just how unique his powers really are

Episode 8 – This Is Frustrating

While exploring his abilities, Jinwoo encounters stronger enemies and uncovers new features of the system, including class upgrades and daily penalties.

Episode 9 – You’ve Been Hiding Your Skills

Jinwoo surprises veteran hunters during a raid with overwhelming strength. Suspicion begins to rise, but Jinwoo keeps his powers hidden for now.

Episode 10 – What Is This, a Picnic?

The Hunters’ Guild investigates Jinwoo, while he dives deeper into more dangerous dungeons, seeking more experience and clues about the system’s origin.

Episode 11 – A Knight’s Oath

Jinwoo faces a powerful boss and earns a major reward: his first summoned shadow soldier. He begins forming his own army from fallen enemies.

Episode 12 – Arise

Jinwoo embraces his true potential as a Shadow Monarch in the making. With newfound confidence, he prepares to challenge the strongest dungeons yet.
